Key Features

Onboarding

Our courses are designed to cater to learners at every stage, from beginners to advanced practitioners. Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen your expertise, you'll find a variety of courses tailored to meet your needs.

Courses

Our multi-modal immersive classroom provides an innovative learning environment that combines various forms of media and interactive tools to create a dynamic and engaging educational experience. This approach ensures that learners can fully immerse themselves in the content, enhancing both understanding and retention.

Practice & Evaluation

Our courses offer a unique practice system that integrates bilingual support and pinyin hints, helping learners master both the language and the subject content more effectively. This approach is especially beneficial for learners studying Chinese or other languages that utilize non-Latin scripts.

Learning Record

Our growth curve visualization feature provides learners with a clear, visual representation of their progress over time. By tracking key performance metrics and displaying them in an intuitive graph, learners can easily see their improvement and identify areas where further development is needed.

Community

Our platform facilitates meaningful interaction with language partners, creating an immersive, collaborative environment where learners can practice speaking, listening, and cultural exchange in real-time. This peer-based approach accelerates language acquisition and fosters confidence in communication.
